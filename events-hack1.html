EWD Hackathon!

 07/19/2014 @FINRA MD Campus

Overview

In December 2013, FINRA's test automation team open-sourced our ExtWebDriver tool. In the months since, we have had the opportunity to present to meetup groups in DC and New York, as well as roll out 4 updates. At this hackathon, we are looking for an infusion of creativity to take ExtWebDriver to the next level as a useful browser automation tool.

Join us Saturday, July 19 for fun, free food, and a friendly competition!

Rules

1. Teams of 1-3 people will compete for the challenge

2. Each team can work on one or more items

3. An item can be an existing ticket or something completely new

4. Each item will be scored using the following rubric:

Score = Size (1-3) * [Completeness (0-15) + Creativity (0-10) + Documentation (0-5)]

5. Grand prize winner will be the team with the highest total score

6. Have fun!

7. Network!
