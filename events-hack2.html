DataGenerator Hackathon

 11/15/2014 @FINRA MD Campus

Overview

DataGenerator 2.0 is here. This version represents a 9-month, complete rewrite based on open standards and Big Data scalability. Join the DataGenerator team at our Hackathon to learn about the latest features and apply the tool to new and interesting problems. Cash prizes will be given to teams enhancing the tool in the most interesting and impactful ways.

Join us Saturday, November 15th for fun, free food, and a friendly competition. Registration will close on Friday, November 14th.

Rules

1. Self-formed teams can be of any size. (Ideally less than 5 people per team)

2. Each team can work on one or more ideas.

3. An idea can be usability/performance/feature enhancements, new features, application of DataGenerator to solve hard practical problems, ingenious ways to market and monetize it as a product, improve the code quality, or something completely new. The goal is to be creative.

4. Each implemented idea will be scored using the following criteria:
     Implementation 40%
     Creativity 50%
     Presentation 10%
     Weighted from 1-3 for the size of the idea implemented.
     NOTE: Un-implemented ideas will not be scored.

5. The theme prize winner will be the team with the best scored idea for a particular theme.

6. The grand prize winner will be the team with best idea across all ideas. There is a high likelihood that the grand prize winner will also be a theme prize winner.
